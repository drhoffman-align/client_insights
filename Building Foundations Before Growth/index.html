🔍 Understanding Key Concepts

Fight or Flight Response

What it is: Operating from a place of urgency or anxiety, making reactive decisions to avoid perceived threats (e.g., financial shortfalls), rather than strategic, planned actions

Foundation vs. Growth

Foundation: Stable, repeatable systems and satisfied clients

Growth: Expanding beyond that

The risk: Skipping foundation leads to instability

Barriers

What they are: Anything that makes it harder to follow through on intentions

Example: Having to get up to get water for meds

The solution: Removing barriers increases success

"Remove any barriers again because sleep is highly important, but everybody always wants to stay asleep."

Plateau vs. Peak (Medication)

Peak: Initial boost from Adderall

Plateau: Steady state after initial effect

Why this matters: Timing matters for optimal functioning

💊 Medication Plan

Adderall 10mg - MORNING BOOST

Timing: Immediately upon waking (5 AM)

Purpose: Provides initial boost to help wake up and start the day

How to take: Place pill and water by bed; take as soon as alarm goes off

Expected benefits: Increased alertness, easier transition out of bed, improved focus for morning routine

Side effects to monitor: Insomnia if taken too late, appetite suppression, jitteriness

Safety warnings: Do not double dose; take only as prescribed

Rationale: Quick onset helps overcome morning inertia

Vyvanse - SUSTAINED FOCUS

Timing: 10-11 AM

Purpose: Extends focus and productivity into late morning/afternoon

How to take: Take with water, after Adderall has peaked

Expected benefits: Sustained attention, smoother energy throughout the day

Side effects to monitor: Same as Adderall; monitor for overlap or excessive stimulation

Safety warnings: Do not take too late in the day

Rationale: Staggered dosing prevents afternoon crash

Temazepam - SLEEP SUPPORT

Timing: 9:30 PM

Purpose: Supports falling asleep on schedule

How to take: Take with water, get in bed immediately after

Expected benefits: Easier sleep onset, improved sleep quality

Side effects to monitor: Next-day drowsiness, dependence if used long-term

Safety warnings: Do not combine with alcohol or other sedatives

Rationale: Helps reset sleep-wake cycle
